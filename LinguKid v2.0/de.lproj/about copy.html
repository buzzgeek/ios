Über diese App

Little English School is a simple learning tool to help small children become acquainted with English vocabulary. In my experience, young children learn most readily in the context of playing, when they don’t feel pressured to work. The concept of Little English School is simple: friendly, inviting pictures are used to entice your child to playfully explore the world of language.

Über mich

I am an English teacher and a mother of two children. I have taught English to all different age groups, from preschool children to adults learning business. The young children I taught really enjoyed learning in connection with colorful, fun pictures, songs, games and crafts. I was often surprised at the progress made by small children who had been learning and playing with me for one or two years, showing me the value of repetitive, passive learning for those young ones.

I had fun creating Little English School – I hope you’ll have fun using it! If you have any questions or comments, please contact me via my website:
